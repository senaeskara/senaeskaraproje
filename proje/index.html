About Women in STEM

Women have made remarkable contributions to Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics (STEM) fields. This website celebrates their achievements and highlights inspiring stories of women breaking barriers in STEM.

Inspiring Women in STEM

Marie Curie

Marie Curie was the first woman to win a Nobel Prize and is famous for her pioneering research on radioactivity.

Katherine Johnson

Katherine Johnson was a NASA mathematician whose calculations were critical to the success of the Apollo missions.
